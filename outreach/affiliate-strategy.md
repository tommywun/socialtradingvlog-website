But partners who offer exclusive deals for our users get a highlighted "Special Offer" badge on their listing. This could be: reduced spreads for the first month, bonus deposit match, free trades for 30 days, etc. Our users get a better deal, you get more signups, we earn our commission.

I get $450 CPA from eToro. I have 333 YouTube videos, 261K views on my top video, professional comparison tools in 10 languages, and a fee calculator that pre-qualifies users before they click through. I'm not sending cold traffic — I'm sending people who've already compared platforms and chosen yours.

We're expanding beyond eToro-focused content into broader trading platform coverage. The site is growing — new SEO articles publishing regularly, calculator tools in 10 languages, and a comparison tool that features the top platforms in each market. Your platform will appear alongside the biggest names in the industry, with real fee data helping users make informed decisions.

## Platform Finder Tool (planned — potential new homepage)

### Core concept
Interactive wizard that takes complete beginners through a personalised journey to find the best platform for them. Not a static comparison table — a guided experience with real data.

### Design principles
1. **Tom's voice throughout** — not corporate disclaimers. Every safety note sounds like a friend warning you, not a legal team covering themselves.
   - NOT: "Trading carries risk. Only invest what you can afford to lose."
   - YES: "Only invest money you can afford to lose — seriously, it's exciting and an amazing opportunity, but the risk is real. Watch my vid to see more about this →"
2. **Educational at every step** — whenever a concept might be scary or confusing (spread, leverage, CFDs, overnight fees), embed a short explainer video or tooltip in plain English
3. **Genuinely useful, not salesy** — show real scraped fee data, real user ratings, real hidden fee warnings. Affiliate links earn commission but recommendations are based on data
4. **Country-aware** — auto-detect location, show only platforms available in their market, in their language
5. **Beginner-first** — jargon-free by default, with "learn more" expansions for people who want detail

### User flow
Step 1: Where are you? What's your experience level? What's your budget? (with personal safety note + video)
Step 2: What interests you? (stocks, crypto, copy trading, ETFs etc — each with explainer)
Step 3: What matters most? (lowest fees, best app, easiest to use, copy trading, safety)
Results: Top 3 personalised matches with match score, total cost simulation, hidden fee alerts, regulation info, user ratings, demo account availability, and Tom's take on each

### Key features
- "What would this trade cost me?" — interactive calculator in results
- Head-to-head comparison (drag any two platforms)
- Risk profile quiz
- "What happens if the market drops 20%?" scenarios
- Country-specific tax tips (ISA in UK, PEA in France etc.)
- Every platform shows required risk disclaimer percentages
- Transparency note: "We earn commission if you sign up — but our data is real"

### Fee Comparison Tool Enhancement
Plan to scrape real fees from all featured platforms weekly:
- Fixed fees (commissions, withdrawal, conversion, inactivity) — published on fee pages
- Overnight/swap rates — published daily
- Typical/minimum spreads — published per instrument
- Top 3 assets per class: stocks, indices, commodities, crypto, ETFs
- Side-by-side trade cost simulation
- "Last updated: [date]" badge
- Disclaimer: fees from public sources, may vary by account type/region
