Frequently Asked Questions

Why do most eToro traders lose money?

The main reasons are over-leveraging, chasing quick profits, poor risk management, and emotional decision-making. eToro's platform makes trading feel simple, which can lead beginners to underestimate the risks of CFDs and leverage.

What percentage of eToro traders are profitable?

According to eToro's own risk disclosure, around 76% of retail investor accounts lose money when trading CFDs. Only roughly 24% of traders are consistently profitable on the platform.

How can I avoid losing money on eToro?

Tom's advice: avoid high leverage, use the virtual practice account first, copy only lower-risk traders with consistent long-term returns, and never invest more than you can afford to lose. Treat it as a long-term investment, not a get-rich-quick scheme.
