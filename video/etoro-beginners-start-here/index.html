Frequently Asked Questions

Is eToro good for complete beginners?

eToro is one of the more beginner-friendly trading platforms due to its copy trading feature, social elements, and clean interface. However, beginners should still understand the risks of CFDs, leverage, and spread fees before depositing real money.

How does eToro work for beginners?

eToro allows you to invest in stocks, crypto, and ETFs, or copy the trades of other investors automatically using the CopyTrader feature. You can start with a free virtual practice account before using real money.

What is the minimum deposit for eToro?

The minimum deposit on eToro is $50 for most countries (though this varies by region). The minimum to copy a trader is $200.
