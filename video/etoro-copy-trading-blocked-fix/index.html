Frequently Asked Questions

Why does eToro say copy trading is blocked?

Copy trading can be blocked for several reasons: the trader has reached their maximum number of copiers, they've voluntarily disabled copying on their account, they're in a region where copying is restricted, or there's a temporary platform issue. Check the trader's profile for any notice about copy availability.

How do I fix 'copy trading blocked' on eToro?

First, check if the trader still accepts copiers on their profile page. If copying is temporarily unavailable, you may need to wait or choose a different trader. If the issue seems like a platform error, try refreshing, clearing cache, or contacting eToro support.

Can all eToro traders be copied?

No. Traders can opt out of being copied, reach a maximum copier limit, or be restricted from copying in certain regions. Only traders who have opted in and have available copy capacity can be copied. The Popular Investor section shows traders who actively participate in copy trading.
