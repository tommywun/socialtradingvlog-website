Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What we collect

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use this site. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time on page
  • Scroll depth and click interactions
  • Device type, browser, and country (anonymised)
  • Referral source (how you found us)

Analytics data is only collected if you click Accept on the consent banner. If you decline, no tracking occurs.

What we don't collect

  • No advertising cookies or remarketing
  • No data sold or shared with third parties
  • No personalised ad tracking
  • No fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is stored with Resend (our email service). You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email.

Contact form

The contact form is powered by Formspree. Your message and email are sent to us directly and not shared with anyone else.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links (primarily to eToro). Clicking them does not share your personal data with us — the affiliate partner tracks the referral using their own cookies, subject to their own privacy policy.

Your rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access — request what data we hold about you
  • Delete — request deletion of your data
  • Correct — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent — change your analytics preference at any time using the "Manage cookies" link in the footer

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact tom@socialtradingvlog.com.
